Welcome to Cannamei

Cannamei is currently under construction, our website is dedicated to the discussion of medical cannabis

We don't remove posts, ban or censor people because they hold a different opinion or are new to the discussion of medical cannabis

Cannamei will be a lightly moderated forum. Extremely overmoderated forums such as reddit censor people for little to no reason. Being a member of this forum means you won't have to worry about overzealous moderators removing your posts or you being shadow banned without your knowledge.

Cannamei is open source, open discussion and a place for everybody

If you would like to help work on Cannamei, please send an email to: info@cannamei.com
